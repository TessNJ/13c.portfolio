Motivation

For this project, focusing on fonts was the main goal. I wanted to go with some simple fonts, a thicker display font for headings and a thinner sans serif font for the body font. Buttons and links also uses the same sans serif, to stay consistant.

I went with these choises, as I know fonts can play a bigger part in setting the tone of a design, but if too agressive can take away from the content of the site.

Lastly I decided to go with a very simple card structure for the cards. Starting with the visual aspect, the written content, and lastly links/buttons to further information.
